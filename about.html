About Us

Our Story

At Pink Muse, scent is an expression of soul. Born from a love for delicate yet daring aromas, our brand captures moments, moods, and memories in every bottle. Founded in 2025, Pink Muse was created to craft perfumes that linger — not just on skin, but in hearts. Each fragrance is inspired by elegance, femininity, and the soft strength of those who wear them.
With carefully chosen ingredients, artful composition, and a touch of magic, our perfumes are designed to be your signature — the final detail in your story.
Every bottle of Pink Muse holds more than just fragrance; it holds intention. We believe perfume is deeply personal — a silent language that speaks before words, that lingers after you leave. Whether it's a whisper of jasmine at dawn or the bold embrace of amber at dusk, our scents are crafted to match your moods, moments, and milestones.
Driven by passion, guided by nature, and perfected by design, Pink Muse is more than a perfume — it's an experience. An invitation to feel more deeply, to express more freely, and to celebrate yourself unapologetically.
Because you're not just wearing a fragrance.

You're wearing Confidence.
You're wearing Power.
You're wearing Pink Muse.
